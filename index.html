Welcome to Leo's Salon

Located in the luxurious Two Rivers Mall, Leo's Salon has been providing premium beauty services in Nairobi for over 7 years. Our 4.6-star rating from 231 reviews speaks to our commitment to excellence.

Our team of skilled stylists and beauty professionals are dedicated to ensuring each client leaves feeling beautiful and pampered. From hair coloring to facials, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet all your beauty needs.
